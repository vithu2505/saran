Artist. Creator. Visionary.

With over five years of mastery in the beauty and craft world, Saran Kirushna built GLÓ from a simple passion into Chennai's most sought-after beauty studio. Her obsession with perfection and her sensitivity to each client's unique aesthetic set her apart.

From breathtaking bridal transformations to hand-stitched Aari masterpieces and one-of-a-kind resin creations, every piece is an act of devotion.

GLÓ exists because every woman deserves to feel radiant — not just on her wedding day, but on every day she chooses to celebrate herself.
